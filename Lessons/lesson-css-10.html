Transitions & Animations

Add motion and interactivity to your website with transitions and animations!

Transitions:

Smooth changes between property values:

  • transition-property - What to animate
  • transition-duration - How long
  • transition-timing-function - ease, linear, ease-in, ease-out

Shorthand:

transition: all 0.3s ease;

Animations:

Create complex, repeating animations with keyframes!

Try It Yourself:

Hover over the boxes to see the effects!
