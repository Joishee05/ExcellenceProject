HTML Entities & Special Characters

What are HTML Entities?

HTML entities are special codes used to display reserved characters, symbols, and characters that don't appear on your keyboard.

Common HTML Entities

  • &lt; - Less than (<)
  • &gt; - Greater than (>)
  • &amp; - Ampersand (&)
  • &quot; - Quotation mark (")
  • &copy; - Copyright symbol (©)
  • &nbsp; - Non-breaking space
