Flexbox

Flexbox is a powerful layout tool that makes arranging elements easy!

Flexbox Container Properties:

  • display: flex; - Enable flexbox
  • flex-direction - row, column, row-reverse, column-reverse
  • justify-content - Horizontal alignment (flex-start, center, space-between, etc.)
  • align-items - Vertical alignment (flex-start, center, stretch, etc.)
  • gap - Space between items
Flexbox Concept: The container is the parent, and items inside are the children!
